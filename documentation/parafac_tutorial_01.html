Hold up! How does your data look?

Ready, right? Not quite! There's a simple rule that applies in computer science: GIGO, or garbage in, garbage out. We can't expect a representation of our data to look reasonable if the algorithm is fed unreasonable data. We need to take a look at the data first if we have not done so already.
tbx.viewabsorbance(samples)
